About Color Rush

Bounce, match colors, and survive the rush!

Game Overview

Color Rush is a fast-paced color-matching reflex game designed to challenge your timing and concentration. Your goal is simple: control a bouncing ball and guide it through colorful rotating obstacles – but only when your ball’s color matches the obstacle!

Key Features

  • 🎯 One-tap control: Easy to learn, hard to master
  • 🎨 Dynamic color-switching gameplay
  • 🌀 Unique rotating shapes and moving obstacles
  • ⚡ Difficulty increases over time – stay sharp!
  • 🌈 Particle effects and smooth animations
  • 🎁 Optional rewarded ads for extra lives
  • 🏆 High score tracking – beat your best run!
  • 📱 Play offline – no internet required

Gameplay Style

Perfect for short bursts of fun or long focus sessions. Color Rush is ideal for fans of reflex games like Color Switch, Helix Jump, Stack Ball, and Flappy-style games.

Monetization

Color Rush is completely free to play. It includes banner ads on menus, interstitial ads between sessions, and optional rewarded ads when you want to continue after losing. There are no in-app purchases.
